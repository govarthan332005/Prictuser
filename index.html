📖 How to use

1. Enter your spins

Tap actual roulette numbers (0–36) or use the manual E/O · R/B buttons. We need at least 5 spins; 10+ is recommended.

2. Choose what to predict

Even/Odd · Red/Black · Both — the AI predicts the next outcome.

3. Hit PREDICT

Five AI models (Markov, Pattern, Streak, Bayesian, Cyclic) vote together. You'll see each model's individual call.

4. Confirm the result

After the actual spin, tap Was Correct / Was Wrong — the engine learns from feedback.

📡 Live Track tab

Continuous mode for ongoing sessions — every entry instantly updates the next-round prediction.

⌨ Keyboard shortcuts

0–9 for numbers · E O R B for manual · Enter = predict · ⌫ = undo · Esc = close

⚠ Reality check

Roulette has a built-in house edge. No prediction system can guarantee profit. Use this as pattern analysis & entertainment. Never bet money you can't afford to lose.
