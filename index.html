📖 How to Use

1️⃣ Enter your spins

Tap actual roulette numbers (0–36) or use Quick E/O · R/B buttons. Need 5+ spins; 10+ is recommended.

2️⃣ Pick what to predict

Even/Odd · Red/Black · Both — AI predicts the next outcome.

3️⃣ Hit PREDICT

9 AI models vote together: Markov, Pattern, Streak, Bayesian, Cyclic, Recency, Bias, Sector, Neural.

4️⃣ Confirm result

After the actual spin, tap Was Correct / Was Wrong — the engine learns from your feedback! 🎯

📡 Live Track tab

Continuous mode — every entry instantly refreshes the next-round prediction.

⌨ Keyboard shortcuts

0–9 single digits · E O R B manual · Enter predict · ⌫ undo · Esc close
